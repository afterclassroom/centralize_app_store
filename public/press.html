CENTRAL APP STORE FOR LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

gotoClassroom is proudly partnered with GradeSlam to match top-notch tutors with K-12 students right from your Learning Management System.


TORONTO, ONTARIO – Toronto based gotoClassroom is proudly partnered with GradeSlam (www.gradeslam.org) to match top-notch tutors with K-12 students right from your Learning Management System. Gradeslam’s tools will be available on gotoClassroom’s Central App Store in June 2015.

GradeSlam is the only on-demand online tutoring marketplace for students K-12. By seeking out the top educators around North America, GradeSlam has developed a platform and service that is perfect for any age, even the youngest students. Unlike other tutoring cernters or services, GradeSlam offers both online and in-home tutoring from tutors who are all background checked and screened to ensure safety and security for every family. Truly the next generation of tutoring.

With an advanced technological platform and snazzy look, GradeSlam is dedicated to the success of each and every student. With GradeSlam you get personalized service and always have someone you can speak to anytime you have a question. GradeSlam is completely committed to making sure families are satisfied, academic needs are met and their expectations are surpassed!

GradeSlam has won numerous awards from the likes of TechCrunch, Caisse Desjardins, McGill University, Notable.ca, and the Quebec Entrepreneurship Contest.

To learn more about GradeSlam’s tools, please visit their website at www.gradeslam.org.
